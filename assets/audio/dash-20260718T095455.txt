The mistake that burns your ad budget every month. Most businesses launch Meta ads without a clear funnel, and wonder why nobody buys. Here is how it actually works. First, you pick one goal: leads, sales, or traffic. Then you define your audience by interest, location, and behavior. Meta finds people who match. Next, your ad shows up in their feed, stories, or reels. The creative has three seconds to stop the scroll. A strong hook, a clear benefit, and one call to action. That is the formula. Start with five dollars a day, test three creatives, and cut the losers fast. Double down on what converts. Your next client is already scrolling. Make sure they stop on your ad.
