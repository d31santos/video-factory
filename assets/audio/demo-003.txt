Your AI can draft a clinical note in seconds. That speed is real, and it is worth using. But speed is not the same as safety. AI still misses context. It misses the offhand comment, the subtle symptom, the thing that only matters because of what happened last visit. So keep one simple habit. Before that note goes anywhere, a human reads it. Ten seconds. You are not re-doing the work. You are catching the one gap that would have cost you an hour, or worse. Draft with AI, ship with a human. That is the whole trick. Follow for safe AI workflows you can actually trust.
